Air Conditioning Services

1. Hi-wall split AC- (Fixed Speed and Inverter)

Split AC means the air conditioning system is made up of two separate units. The air-cooled condensing unit called the compressor unit is placed outside the air-conditioned area whereas the air-handling unit is inside the room where the AC is fixed.

2. Window AC

These are the basic models available in both cooling and heating as well as cooling only. They require comparably large space to fit in( generally in windows) and spaces for them are usually given at construction level only. Window ACs are available in two types:

a) Inverter Window ACs

An inverter air conditioner is an air conditioner with variable cooling speed. These systems provide high-efficiency cooling performance without all the strain or cost of a standard air conditioning system.

b) Fixed speed window ACs

Fixed speed air conditioners, also known as standard or non-inverter, present a straightforward, single speed motor operation: on and off. Once the desired temperature is reached, they turn off, turning back on only when the temperature rises to a set level.

3. AHU

An air handling unit, commonly called an AHU, is the composition of elements mounted in large, accessible box-shaped units called modules, which house the appropriate ventilation requirements for purifying, air-conditioning or renewing the indoor air in a building or premises.

4. Package AC

It's an evolved version of window ACs. In this, the cool air is transferred through air ducts to different places. The air ventilations are usually hidden in the ceilings and walls of the building. The outdoors is placed in a room or on the rooftops.
