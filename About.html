About us

Since 2015, The Best Air Conditioning Service In Our City.

In 2015, when the company was incorporated, the owners and present day directors of the company were committed to give its customers superior quality products along with the best in class after sale services.

The dream was big, but achievable. The knowledge and skills they had about the line of products and business model, helped them carve a business that's running successfully now. The relationships we build with the customers are long lasting and is the result of our fabulous team working round the clock everyday.

Mission

We, At Refricon Services, Aim To Change The Service Experience In The HVAC (Heating, Ventilation And Air Conditioning) Section For The Customers.

We provide a one stop solution for all HVAC related queries. We believe
and focus more on a long term trust building with the customers we serve rather than giving them a one time service. We are committed to solving every query our customers raise.

Vision

Launched With A Vision To Revolutionize The After Sale Service Experience For The Customers,Refricon Services Has Been Very Much Successful To Fulfill Their Vision.

It has built such incredible customer support that customers feel more connected and can rely on the service we provide to them. With a handful of experienced service men, we are all set to expand and create more customer base.
